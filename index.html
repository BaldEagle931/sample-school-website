About Our High School

Here at Aurora Primary and High School, we focus firmly on academics, behaviour, punctuality, cleanliness. We also arrange and inspire our students to take part in extracurricular activities. Our honorable teachers and principal guide the students with care while firmly enforcing the rules. Here students are provided with a very friendly learning and developing environment.

Academics

We provide academic courses from Class-I to Class-X.

Admissions

Admission takes place every year from January to February. Applicants must sit in an exam and an interview with a board of teachers to be selected.
